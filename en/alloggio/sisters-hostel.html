About the property

The Sisters' Hostel – Women Only Hostel is a hostel located in Adeje, about 900 metres from Playa del Bobo and 300 metres from Aqualand. The property features a garden and free WiFi, in a convenient position close to some of the main points of interest in southern Tenerife.

A hostel designed exclusively for women guests, in a tidy and quiet environment — ideal for those travelling solo or in a group, looking for comfort and safety near the south's attractions.

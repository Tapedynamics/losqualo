About the property

An exclusive penthouse in the heart of Los Cristianos, one of the liveliest and most complete resort towns in southern Tenerife. Set on the top floor of a modern building, this penthouse offers a 180-degree panoramic view over the sea, the harbour and the Atlantic sunset — a spectacle that renews itself every evening.

The interiors are furnished with luxury finishes, fine materials and a contemporary design that combines elegance and comfort. The rooftop terrace is the heart of the property: a private space to relax, enjoy breakfast with a sea view or savour drinks and nibbles at sunset.

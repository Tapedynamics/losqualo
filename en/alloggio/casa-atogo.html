About the property

Casa Atogo is an independent house set in the peaceful countryside of southern Tenerife, designed as a holiday home for families and groups looking for authenticity, comfort and generous spaces.

Just 5 minutes from the South Airport, the location guarantees privacy and quiet while remaining strategically placed for easily reaching the island's main areas.

It is the ideal choice for those who want to experience Tenerife in a relaxed way, away from the nightlife but without isolation.

About the property

Villa Paraiso is a modern property in a prime location within the exclusive resort of Playa Paraíso, set over two levels to accommodate up to 20 people (10 per floor).

Ideal for large groups, organised tour-operator holidays, corporate retreats and private events, the villa offers contemporary interiors and generous spaces for an unforgettable stay.

Thanks to its smart layout and high-quality services, Villa Paraiso delivers a refined experience suited to both extended families and the most demanding business-to-business clients.

About the property

Blue Paradise CLVNG is a modern coliving house in the Ifara barrio of Santa Cruz de Tenerife, designed for digital nomads, remote workers and young adults looking for a vibrant community in a healthy, organised environment.

A cross between a private home and a smart hostel: cosy rooms with tropical names (Banana, Mango, Papaya, Pineapple, Star Fruit, Pitaya, Beetroot Suite), an integrated coworking space and clear rules to guarantee quality of life.

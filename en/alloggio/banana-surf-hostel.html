About the property

Banana Surf Hostel is a surf hostel in the heart of Los Cristianos, in the south of Tenerife, just minutes from the beach and the harbour. Accommodation designed for travellers looking for comfort, proximity to the sea and new friendships.

A space where calm and sociability meet: the atmosphere is welcoming and informal, ideal for surfers, travellers and groups who want to experience the island in good company.

Experiences & services

🏄 Surf

Surf lessons and easy access to the south coast spots: the property was born with a surf house soul.

🎉 Group activities

Daily activities and shared experiences to meet other travellers and enjoy the island together.

🌋 Excursions

Organised excursions, including trips to Teide, to discover the most iconic landscapes of Tenerife.

⛴️ Inter-island transfers

Ferry transfers to the other Canary Islands, for those who want to extend their trip beyond Tenerife.

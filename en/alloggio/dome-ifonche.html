Description of the experience

The Dome Experience is a unique experience that combines nature, relaxation and comfort in a striking rural setting. Set within the beautiful natural surroundings of Finca Tres Roques (between Ifonche and Vilaflor), it offers the chance to sleep in a private geodesic dome.

Surrounded by the tranquillity of the inland hills, with views over the surrounding landscape and the starry sky above you.

Why choose it

🌿 Connection with nature 🌌 Night sky views 🔒 Private atmosphere 💕 Perfect for couples 🥾 Rural excursions add-on

Accommodation details

🏕️ The Dome

  • Private structure for 2 people
  • King-size bed with panoramic view
  • Full, modern bathroom
  • Minimalist design with natural materials
  • View of the night sky

📍 Location

The Finca is set in a rural area in Vilaflor/Ifonche, surrounded by vineyards and unspoilt nature, with panoramic views towards the mountains and the southern coast.

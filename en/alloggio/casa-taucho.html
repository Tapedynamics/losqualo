About the property

Casa Dolores is an independent rural house located in the Taucho area – Tenerife South, set in the peaceful countryside with open views over the ocean and La Gomera.

It is an ideal choice for groups of up to 10 people seeking privacy, outdoor spaces and an authentic setting, away from the nightlife but well connected to the main areas of the island's south.

The house is prepared and arranged according to the number of confirmed guests, ensuring comfort and efficient use of the spaces.

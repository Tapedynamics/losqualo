About the property

Hotel Rural Arona is a charming rural accommodation surrounded by nature, offering an authentic experience deeply rooted in the history of Tenerife.

The property was created from an old Canarian pajar, today recognised as a "Bien de Interés Cultural" and "Patrimonio Histórico", having preserved its original Canarian architecture dating back to 1720.

Every space tells the story of the island's traditional rural life, reinterpreted with a contemporary sensibility to offer comfort, silence and a strong sense of connection with the land.

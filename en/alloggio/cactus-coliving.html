About the property

Cactus Coliving is a network of three coliving and coworking spaces in the Canary Islands: two in Tenerife (Valle San Lorenzo and Adeje, both in the south) and one on the island of La Gomera. The concept blends remote work, community and immersion in the Canaries' volcanic nature.

The "Live & Work in the Canary Islands" philosophy translates into an integrated offering: spaces designed for productivity, shared moments (family dinners, workshops, yoga, excursions) and direct access to beaches, trails and volcanic views. High-speed internet and a welcoming atmosphere are confirmed by the reviews.

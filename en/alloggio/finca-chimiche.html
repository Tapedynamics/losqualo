About the property

A private finca designed exclusively as a couples' getaway, where the experience focuses on tranquillity, the outdoor jacuzzi and the open views of southern Tenerife.

A private cabaña with an indoor space equipped for couples, surrounded by a natural setting with views over the sea and the volcanic landscape of the south. You can personalise your stay with decoration and additional services.

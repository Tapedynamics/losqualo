About the property

The Surf House Rurale is the Casa Rural – Atogo: a detached house set in the peaceful countryside of southern Tenerife, offered in the Surf House format for groups of surfers and families looking for generous spaces, privacy and rural comfort.

Just 5 minutes from the South Airport, the location guarantees privacy and quiet while staying strategically placed to easily reach El Médano and the surf spots in the south of the island.

How to stay

The Surf House Rurale adapts to different travel styles and budgets, with four booking options:

🛏️ Hostel — Bed

A bed in a shared room. Ideal for solo surfers or small groups who want to save and share the experience.

  • Rooms with 4–6 beds
  • Shared common areas
  • Pool and garden included

🚪 Room — Private

Private room with access to the common areas. Available in double, triple, quadruple or 6-bed configuration.

  • Double, triple or multiple room
  • Shared or private bathroom
  • Kitchen, pool and outdoor areas included

🏡 House — Exclusive

The entire property exclusively for your group or family. Maximum privacy, full spaces and no compromises.

  • 5 bedrooms, up to 12–14 people
  • Exclusive use of pool and jacuzzi
  • Ideal for families and organised groups

🏄 Surf Camp / Retreat

Dedicated partner rates for surf camp organisers, surf schools and sports or wellness retreats.

  • Terms reserved for partners and operators
  • Option of packages combined with lessons
  • Logistics coordination with Lo Squalo

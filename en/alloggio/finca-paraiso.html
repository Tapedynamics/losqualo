Description

A rustic-chic finca full of charm, set in the natural surroundings of Playa Paraiso, one of the most striking stretches of Tenerife's south-west coast. Designed to celebrate extraordinary moments: weddings, ceremonies, corporate retreats and group gatherings in a setting of rare beauty.

The well-kept tropical gardens, the outdoor event spaces and the panoramic view over the Atlantic Ocean create a natural backdrop of luxury that no artificial venue could match. Every corner tells a story, every sunset becomes a memory.

Location

📍 Playa Paraiso, Adeje

  • Exclusive area on the Adeje coast
  • 20 minutes from Tenerife South Airport
  • Close to the beaches of Playa Paraiso and La Enramada
  • Easy access for guests coming from all over the world

Features

🌵 Event spaces

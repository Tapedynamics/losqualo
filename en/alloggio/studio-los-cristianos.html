About the property

Cosy studio set in the heart of Los Cristianos, one of the most authentic and lively spots in South Tenerife. An area that preserves the soul of the old fishing village, today perfectly integrated with amenities, beaches and transport links.

It is the ideal choice for those who want a relaxing, practical car-free holiday, with the sea, the restaurants and local daily life just a few steps away.

The area: authentic Los Cristianos

Los Cristianos is one of the few places in Tenerife where you can still feel the real life of the island:

🌅 Local atmosphere

  • Seafront walks alongside the locals
  • Historic bars, markets and local restaurants
  • Marina with connections to La Gomera
  • Calm yet lively atmosphere all year round

📍 Strategic location

  • Serviced beaches and natural coves nearby
  • Supermarkets, bakeries, restaurants
  • Excellent bus and taxi connections
  • Easy access to Las Américas and Calle México

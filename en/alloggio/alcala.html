About the property

Set in the heart of Alcalá, one of the most authentic coastal villages in southern Tenerife, this apartment is the ideal choice for those who want to experience the island in a genuine way, away from the nightlife and mass tourism.

Alcalá still preserves the atmosphere of a real Canarian village: fishermen, lively squares, local bars, traditional restaurants and a relaxed pace of life, perfect for those seeking peace and quiet without giving up essential amenities.

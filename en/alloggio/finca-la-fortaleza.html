About the property

Set in the natural, authentic surroundings of San Miguel de Abona, Finca La Fortaleza is a private villa designed for groups seeking space, tranquillity and a strategic location in the south of Tenerife.

The finca offers a balanced experience of nature, comfort and accessibility. The property is accessible for people with reduced mobility.

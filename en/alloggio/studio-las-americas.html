About the property

Bright studio set in one of the most central and well-served areas of South Tenerife, in Playa de Las Américas. A perfect choice for those who want the sea, amenities, transport and the main attractions just a few steps away, without giving up comfort.

Thanks to its strategic location, the studio is ideal both as a base for exploring the island and for a relaxing holiday between beach and pool.

Location

📍 Extremely convenient

  • 5-minute walk from Playa de Bobo
  • 2 minutes from the Costa Adeje bus stop
  • Taxis available right next to the building
  • Restaurants, bars and shops very close by
  • A few minutes from Siam Park and Siam Mall

🚶 Without a car

One of the best-connected areas on the island, perfect even without a car. Everything is within walking distance.
